What is Cosmic Yield ?

Cosmic Yield is an innovative blockchain-based space mining investment game that combines idle gameplay mechanics with real financial rewards. Build and upgrade mining facilities across 8 different planet types, manage dual resources (Energy and Plasma), and watch your investment grow through strategic optimization.

Unlike traditional games, Cosmic Yield offers a practice mode where you can learn the mechanics without any wallet connection, making it perfect for beginners. When you're ready, switch to on-chain mode to earn real USDT rewards.

Key Highlights
  • 360 tiles to build your cosmic empire
  • 8 unique planet types with different production rates
  • Dual resource economy: Energy for building, Plasma for profit
  • Daily battle system for bonus rewards
  • Convert Plasma to USDT anytime

Game Mechanics

Planets & Buildings

Your empire consists of mining planets placed on a 360-tile grid. Each planet produces Energy and Plasma every hour. Planets range from basic Mercury facilities to advanced Neptune stations, with higher-level planets costing more but producing significantly better yields.

Dual Resources System

Energy (‚ö°) - Used for building new planets and upgrading existing ones. Earned through daily production and battle rewards. Cannot be withdrawn.

Plasma (üíé) - Your profit currency that can be converted to USDT. Earned through daily production from your planets. Can be swapped to Energy at a 1:2 ratio for faster expansion.

Upgrades

Each planet can be upgraded up to 9 levels (Level 1 through Level 10). Each upgrade costs 25% of the planet's base cost and increases production by 25% of the base rate. Upgrades are a cost-effective way to boost production without using more tiles.

Upgrade Example

A Mercury planet costs 10,000 Energy and produces 4/hour. Upgrading to Level 2 costs 2,500 Energy and adds +1/hour production. By Level 10, the same planet produces 13/hour.

Swap System

Strategic resource swapping is key to optimal growth. Convert Plasma to Energy at a 1:2 rate when you need to expand quickly. Be smart on reinvestment to minimize your time to ROI.

Battle System

Engage in daily battles to earn bonus Energy. Battle rewards are calculated based on your total production rate (perHour √ó 12.8), meaning the more you produce, the bigger your daily battle bonus. This creates a powerful compound growth effect.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start earning ?
First, try Practice Mode to learn the game mechanics. When ready, connect your wallet in On-Chain mode on BSC Blockchain (you can add it on chainlist.org), invest USDT to get Energy, build planets, and start earning Plasma. Convert Plasma to USDT anytime to realize your profits.
What's the minimum investment ?
The minimum investment is 10 USDT, which gives you 10,000 Energy - enough to start building your first planets.
How long until I reach ROI ?
ROI timing depends on your investment size and reinvestment strategy. Use the ROI Calculator to simulate different scenarios. Generally, with optimal smart reinvestment, most players reach ROI within 45-60 days.
Can I withdraw my Plasma anytime ?
Yes ! Convert your Plasma to USDT and withdraw at any time. There's no lock-up period. However, keeping your earnings invested and using smart reinvestment will maximize your long-term returns.
What's the difference between Energy and Plasma ?
Energy is your building currency - use it to construct and upgrade planets. Plasma is your profit currency that converts to USDT. Both are produced hourly by your planets at equal rates.
How do battles work ?
Once per day, you can engage in a battle that rewards you with bonus Energy (approximately 12.8√ó your hourly production rate). Higher production means bigger battle rewards, creating a compound growth effect.
What blockchain is Cosmic Yield on ?
Cosmic Yield will launch on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), offering fast transactions and low fees. Practice Mode works offline without any blockchain interaction.

Tokenomics

Economy Overview

Cosmic Yield operates on a dual-token economy designed for sustainability and growth. Energy circulates within the game ecosystem for building and upgrading, while Plasma represents real value that can be extracted as USDT.

Energy Supply

Energy enters the system through USDT deposits (1 USDT = 1,000 Energy) and battle rewards. It exits through building costs and upgrade costs. The swap system allows Energy to be created from Plasma at a 1:2 ratio, enabling strategic liquidity.

Plasma Economics

Plasma is generated through hourly production from planets. Players can convert Plasma to USDT (1,000 Plasma = 1 USDT) or swap to Energy for expansion. The system is designed so early investors who use smart reinvestment can achieve strong ROI while maintaining sustainability.

Swap Rates

The 1:2 swap ratio (1 Plasma ‚Üí 2 Energy) creates interesting strategic choices. Converting Plasma to Energy for expansion can accelerate growth but delays profit-taking. The ROI calculator helps determine the optimal balance.

Sustainability Model

The game economy is designed for long-term sustainability. New player investments fund withdrawals of existing players, similar to how early investors in growing platforms benefit from network growth. The smart reinvestment system and efficiency-based building encourage healthy ecosystem growth.
